## Summary

The [codrops/SlideshowAnimations](https://github.com/codrops/SlideshowAnimations) repository is licensed under the **MIT License** (copyright 2009-2022 Codrops). The code may be freely used in personal and commercial projects. The one obligation is preserving the copyright notice and license text in all copies.

The demo images are credited to Midjourney and are **not** covered by the MIT license. They should not be reused or redistributed. Replace them with your own assets.

### Demo Images: Midjourney

- README states: "Images generated with Midjourney"
- MIT license covers "Software" (code), not creative assets used as demo content
- Midjourney's ToS: paid-plan subscribers own generated images commercially; free-tier output is CC-BY-NC 4.0
- Codrops has not explicitly sublicensed the images to repo consumers
- **Recommendation:** Replace all demo images with your own. Do not reuse or republish the included Midjourney images.

### Codrops General Licensing (tympanus.net/codrops/licensing/)

- Downloadable demos: MIT, same terms as above
- Design freebies: allowed for personal and commercial use; cannot redistribute or sell the items themselves
- Article content: excerpting allowed; full republication and translations require Codrops permission

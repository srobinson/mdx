## WHAT TO SKIP

- **Streaming/content creation** for short-term income (6+ months to first meaningful dollar)
- **Template/starter kit sales** without an existing audience (medium-term play)
- **Open source bounties as primary income** (most underpay; supplementary only)
- **Bug bounty hunting without AI security specialization** (95% quit; 2-3 years to sustainable income)
- **Kaggle competitions** requiring deep ML background (your edge is systems, not ML research)
- **Medium Partner Program** ($5-50/article; not worth it vs. $300-900 paid platform articles)

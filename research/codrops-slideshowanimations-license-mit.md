## Summary

### Codrops General Licensing (tympanus.net/codrops/licensing/)

Codrops downloadable demos use MIT. Their "Design Freebies" category has stricter terms (no redistribution/resale of the freebie itself), but that does not apply to code demos like this one.

### Midjourney Demo Images

The README credits: "Images generated with Midjourney."

The MIT license covers Codrops' code, not third-party-generated assets. Midjourney's Terms of Service govern the images. Rights to those images belong to the Midjourney account holder (Codrops) under Midjourney's ToS -- they are not relicensed to downstream users via MIT. Do not reuse the demo images. Substitute your own assets.

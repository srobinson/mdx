## Executive Summary

A solo developer building AI/ML tooling (Rust, TypeScript, Claude API, Supabase) can realistically access **$50,000 to $200,000+** in aggregate free credits by stacking programs across cloud providers, AI API vendors, database platforms, and developer tool programs. The landscape divides into three tiers: (1) always-free tiers requiring no application, (2) self-serve startup programs requiring a website and business entity, and (3) competitive/curated programs offering the largest credit amounts. Most programs are stackable and non-exclusive.

## Source Quality Assessment

**High confidence**: Cloud provider programs (AWS, GCP, Azure, Oracle), major AI API free tiers (Gemini, Groq, Cerebras, Mistral), and OSS tool programs (JetBrains, 1Password, Sentry) are well-documented with official sources. Credit amounts and eligibility requirements are verified across multiple sources.

**Medium confidence**: Startup credit programs from AI API vendors (Anthropic, OpenAI, Together AI) have less transparent eligibility criteria. Exact credit amounts vary based on application quality and are at provider discretion. The $150/mo xAI data sharing program is well-documented but the irrevocable opt-in is a significant caveat.

**Lower confidence**: Some aggregator sites (freestartupdeals.com, getaiperks.com) may have outdated information on smaller programs. IBM and Alibaba Cloud programs have less recent verification. Reddit yielded essentially zero relevant discussions for this topic.

**Known gaps**: Replicate's current free tier amount is unclear (shifted to prepaid credit model July 2025). Some accelerator programs (AWS GAIA, Vercel AI Accelerator) have specific cohort windows that may not be currently open. Hetzner has no formal developer program beyond referral credits.

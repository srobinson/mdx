## Executive Summary

An exhaustive multi-angle search was conducted to identify a specific website featuring stunning full-page photography of large, boulder-like decorative stones with beautiful matte colors, amazing lighting, and creative layout/transition techniques between photos. After 30+ searches across Awwwards, web design showcases, stone company websites, creative agencies, and design galleries, the exact site was NOT definitively identified. The strongest candidates are cataloged below, ranked by likelihood of match.

## Tier 1: Strongest Candidates (award-winning stone websites with creative gallery design)

### 1. The Quarry House (mfisher-apollonas.com)
- **URL**: https://mfisher-apollonas.com/ and https://mfisher-apollonas.com/gallery
- **Awwwards SOTD** (score ~7.64), plus Developer Award (Dec 1, 2022)
- Built by **CUSP** and **Salt and Pepper** agencies
- Immersive digital experience featuring natural stone photography
- Fan-style gallery layout with parallax effects, 360-degree views, 3D components
- Technologies: WebGL, Three.js, animations
- Categories: Architecture, Art & Illustration, Experimental, Unusual Navigation
- **NOTE**: The domain mfisher-apollonas.com connects to Matthew Fisher (ruled out by user), so this may be the same entity. However, "The Quarry House" is a distinct project from his general "Art Objects" site. Worth confirming with user.

### 2. Formigari (formigari.it)
- **URL**: https://formigari.it/en/
- **Awwwards SOTD**
- Italian artisanal company specializing in natural stone, glazed lava stone, terrazzo since 1972
- Full-screen immersive gallery layout with large background image sections
- Showcases different stone materials: Breccia Elladica, Vanilla glazed lava stone, Incavoline Verde, Cristallo Rosa
- Alternating layouts between text overlays and image sections
- Smooth transitions between product showcases
- Based in Verona with showroom and slab warehouse

### 3. Stone and Style (stoneandstyle.com)
- **URL**: https://stoneandstyle.com/
- **Awwwards SOTD** (score 7.62)
- "Designs and crafts exquisite materials, deeply obsessed with design-led innovations"
- Technologies: WebGL, Three.js, animations
- Mexican stone industry company, factories in Torreon, Coahuila
- Gallery section with projects showcase

### 4. Lundhs Real Stone (lundhsrealstone.com)
- **URL**: https://lundhsrealstone.com/en
- **Awwwards Honorable Mention**
- Norwegian natural stone producer (Larvikite and Anorthosite)
- Sophisticated animations with opacity transitions, "moonwalk" progressive reveal
- Carousel/slider navigation, responsive grid layouts
- Stone varieties with beautiful color names: Blue, Royal, Emerald, Antique, Ocean, Marina, Grey
- Minimalist elegance with ample whitespace
